Introduction

Athlete burnout is a very important issue that affects people of all ages, you can be young, a student athlete, or even a professional athlete. Sports are meant to be fun, help with building teamwork, and even feel a sense of accomplishment. Sports can also cause bad physical and mental stress. A lot of athletes are always exhausted and feel unmotivated which can make them lose the love for the sport they used to have so much fun playing. On this website you can explore all the important factors that can cause an athlete burnout while also looking at the serious effects a burnout can have on mental health and performance. By understanding how a burnout happens, how it affects athletes, and how to prevent it, everyone can help athletes stay healthy and stay loving the sport they play. No matter who you are, learning about burnouts is very important to keeping sports positive.
